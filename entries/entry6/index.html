Outfit of the Day

Octber 8-October 14





































When the temperature in New York plummets;

When sunshine and softness are scarce;

For the perfect winter day,

You'll need an almighty soft sweater;

And you'll feel like you've been kissed by the sun;

Fluffy and light for every afternoon.












Summer halter dress is a high-fashioned item for girls;

The spaghetti straps are perfect for underwear;

Some halter tops also have lace webbing added to the design;

Interspersed with thin ribbons of the same color as the cuttlebutt knot decoration;

And you'll feel like you've been kissed by the sun;

The design is delicate with a touch of playfulness and cuteness.
